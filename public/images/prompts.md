# Image Generation Prompts

**Style**: Ink wash / graphic novel / sketchy. Dark. NOT photorealistic.
**Format**: Generate at 16:9 aspect ratio, save as .webp
**Tool**: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Flux — whichever gives best ink wash style
